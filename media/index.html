Podcast

Tammy Spear on In Defense of Fandom — “Parasocial-Adjacent”

Season 3, Episode 3 · Released May 20, 2025 · Listen on Apple Podcasts

Tammy Spear joins the In Defense of Fandom podcast to talk about parasocial-adjacent attachments — the unguarded, intelligent affection people develop for public figures, and what that affection reveals about the listener. The conversation moves from Taylor Swift fandom to clinical observation about identification, projection, and the legitimacy of caring about strangers.
